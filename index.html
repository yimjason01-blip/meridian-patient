8-week sleep and stress protocol

Your HRV has been trending down for 6 months and resting heart rate crept up. That's an autonomic story, and it's making the cardiac number worse.
What it isSleep extended to 7.5 h minimum. 30-min wind-down (no screens, dim lights, room below 68°F). 10 min breath work daily. Reassess in 8 weeks.

How we'll know it's workingHRV back above 50 ms (4-week average). Resting HR under 55.

Cardiac workup before endurance training climbs

You're pushing hard on the bike. We want a clean look at your coronary arteries before intensity rises further.

How we'll know it's workingThe result is the answer. CAC < 100 → stay the course. Higher → revisit intensity targets together.
